# Areas

Ongoing responsibilities and domains you maintain over time — no end date, no single deliverable.

## Workflow
1. Create a note using the **Area of Knowledge** template for each area.
2. Link related notes, projects, and references.
3. Review periodically to keep information current.

## Examples
- Health & fitness
- Team leadership
- Home finances
- A specific technology you maintain expertise in
